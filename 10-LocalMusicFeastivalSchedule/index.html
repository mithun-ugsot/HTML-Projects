Stage Information

Main Stage
Located at the north end of Riverside Park. Capacity: 5,000. Open-air with full sound and lighting.
Acoustic Tent
Covered tent near the oak grove. Capacity: 500. Intimate setting for unplugged performances.

Festival Information

Tickets & Admission
  • Day Pass: $35
  • Weekend Pass: $60
  • Kids under 12: Free

Gates open at 10:00 AM each day.

Rules & Guidelines
  • No outside alcohol permitted
  • Blankets and lawn chairs welcome
  • No pets except service animals
  • Re-entry allowed with wristband
Food & Drinks

Local food trucks and beverage stands are located along the south pathway. V/VG options available at every vendor.
